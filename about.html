Why Choose Us!

PT Taruna Eka Setia (PT TES) is a company engaged in providing industrial boiler service and rental services. Our company was founded on November 11 2008 in Sidoarjo, East Java. Boiler rental is one of the core areas of our business. Apart from rental, we are engaged in boiler service (troubleshooting, retubing, repair & cleaning).
